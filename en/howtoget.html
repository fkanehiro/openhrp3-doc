Currently, we provide only limited distribution for the user test.

If you want to participate in the user test, please apply to the limited distribution account after you agree to the terms of the program license.

When you apply, please fill in:

  • Name
  • Position
  • Email address
  • Desired account Name, Password
and email the above information to the account services for OpenHRP3 account (openhrp-contact (at) m.aist.go.jp), and then sign the user agreement and send it by mail to the below described address.

Humanoid Research Group,
Intelligent Systems Research Institute, National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology (AIST)
Tukuba Central2, Umezono1-1-1, Tsukuba, Ibaraki 305-8568
OpenHRP3 Account Services

After we receive your email, we will complete your registration and send you a confirmation. You can send the user agreement by mail after completing the email registration. If we don't receive your user agreement within a certain period, your account will be deleted.

Because the number of the accounts is limited, we advise you to apply for an account as soon as possible.

Also, we are planning to open the accounts for general use around next April.

Administrator: OpenHRP Team, Humanoid Research Group, Intelligent Systems Research Institute, AIST
